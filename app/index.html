CDPAP IS A MEDICAID PROGRAM THAT ALLOWS YOU TO CHOOSE WHO PROVIDES YOUR CARE AT NO COST TO YOU

Your friend or relative gets paid for helping you with your day-to-day needs.

The CDPAP Process – It’s Our Job

We’re experienced professionals here to make the CDPAP enrollment process easy. Don’t get overwhelmed by complicated paperwork. Reach out to us today and we’ll get you set up with the care you need.

01
Assessment for Eligibility

We work with you and your insurance company to determine your care needs and eligibility.

We can help you enroll in CDPAP for the first time, or help you make the switch to Buffalo Homecare if you’re already enrolled.

02
Enroll

Once you qualify, we are here to help you with all onboarding and ongoing support.

We handle the paperwork and make sure your caregiver gets paid.

03
Choose Your Caregiver

You choose the relative, friend, or trusted person who you want to be your caregiver.

No training is required, only their willingness to provide you with quality care.

04
You Get Care – They Get Top Pay

You know your needs best. You work with your selected caregiver to get the help you need to improve your quality of life.

We make sure they get paid top dollar.
